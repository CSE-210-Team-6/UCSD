<h1 class="sr-only">Tackling the Wildfire Crisis </h1>
<p class=" ">Increased temperatures, weather extremes and hot and dry conditions have intensified the threat of wildfires in California and around the world. At UC San Diego, researchers across disciplines are working to combat wildfires and the problems that stem from them through novel research, new technologies and critical state and federal partnerships.</p> <a class="btn btn-lg btn-default rt-btn-yellow " data-h1="" data-module="hero-homepage" href="https://today.ucsd.edu/story/alert-california" role="button">Learn about our wildfire research</a> </div>

<h1 class="sr-only">Celebrating Latinx Heritage Month</h1>
<p class=" ">The UC San Diego community is invited to join in the commemoration of Latinx Heritage Month during the month of October. A series of events has been curated to help honor the history, culture and contributions of the Latinx community, including live performances, an inspiring keynote address and film screenings at UC San Diego Park &amp; Market.
